Pipeline Methodology

Deduplication: Each applicant may appear 1–8 times across lenders. Records are normalised (OCR-fix on PAN, strip +91/0 on mobile, multi-format DOB parse), blocked by PAN prefix + DOB year + mobile tail-4, then scored: 0.55 × JaroWinkler(PAN) + 0.25 × mobile_exact + 0.20 × DOB_sim. Pairs ≥ 0.80 are merged via Union-Find transitive closure. Result: 100% precision, 97.7% recall, F1 = 98.8%.

Segmentation: Five cohorts assigned by rule priority — Thin File (avg tradelines < 3) overrides all score bands, then Prime Stable (≥750), Prime Active (700+, multi-lender), Near Prime (600–699), Subprime (<600). Activation score = weighted composite of score recency, score improvement, lender demand, and pull frequency.

Blind-Spot Flags: Three override conditions surface applicants who fail standard underwriting but show positive signals — improving thin files, multi-lender subprime recovery, and near-prime multi-lender demand. About 17–18% of the portfolio is flagged.
